Hello;

My name is Haimanot Gebre; I am the owner and author of "A Key to Mathematics" reference book. I received a bachelor's degree in Applied Mathematics from Gondar University in 1999 E.C and served as a Mathematics and IT teacher for 7 years from 8th-12th grade. Currently I am working in mobile sales and repair business. I am one of the founders of the Gurage Goye Civic Association and I am serving as the secretary general of the association.

I am the founder and owner of Hi-Me Business, which will soon be announced and which consists of 6 sister companies (Hi-Me Mobile, Hi-Me Equb, Hi-Me GDesign, Hi-Me E-Commerce, Hi-Me Tech, Hi-Me Furniture). . I am also a collector and founder of Hi-Me Equb, a graphic artist, a woodworker, a mobile and computer hardware and software repair specialist, and a mobile software repair trainer.

I have a strong desire to become a software engineer and programmer. It is my vision to see Hi-Me Business as an institution that creates job opportunities for many young people with the help of modern practices and technology. In the next 10 years, I have a firm dream to build an internet digital business system that can generate better and continuous income without needing my existence.

My philosophy of life is "Being trustworthy to the creator and himself, a true and honest servant and close to those who need me."
